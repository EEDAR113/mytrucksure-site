The MyTrucksure is a purpose built SaaS app for the trucking insurance market. It is a technology platform for insurance Carriers, MGA's, Brokers and InsureTech's as well as Lloyd's syndicates and Risk Retention Groups. Whether your organization is large or small, it is for anyone who is looking to sell and service insurance to the highly specialized U.S. trucking industry.

MyTrucksure supports a fully digital policy life cycle

Including rate, quote, bind, issue endorsements, renewal and cancellation and out of the box the collection of specific trucking data including owner-operator, carrier, fleet and driver information. The app is connected to a growing ecosystem of trucking specific third party data and services specific to needs of selling and truck insurance including MVR retrieval, state auto liability insurance reporting and premium finance. MyTrucksure powered by InsureMO® is based on latest micro-service, REST API, cloud native technology architecture. It is hosted in the U.S. on Amazon Web Services and is cost effective.

Our Solutions

MyTrucksure offers digital solutions that simplify underwriting, policy administration, billing and payments, and more.

Underwriting

Digital Underwriting Workbench

  • Stand alone intelligent underwriting and automation integrated with advanced data and AI capabilities
  • Purpose-built for trucking, fleet, and commercial auto underwriting
  • Compliments any back-office policy administration system
  • Rating options include: Excel Rating-as-a-Service or InsureMO Rating-as-a-Service
  • Increase submission response rates
  • Powered by InsureMO, cloud native, microservice, API first insurance architecture

Policy Administration

Full Life-Cycle Policy Admin

  • Intelligent underwriting and automation integrated with advanced data and AI capabilities
  • Full life-cycle policy administration system of record (SOR)
  • Rating options include: Excel Rating-as-a-Service or InsureMO Rating-as-a-Service
  • Rapid cost, effective onboarding
  • Powered by InsureMO, cloud native, microservice, API first insurance architecture

Billing and Payments

Integrated billing, collections, and payment opportunities

  • Fully integrated with MyTrucksure Policy Administration
  • Billing and payment information available at the policy level for underwriters or for back-office billing and payment analysts
  • DirectBill, Agent Bill, and multiple installment plan options, taxes, fees, and miscellaneous fee calculation
  • Secure, real-time and scheduled payment integrations and Premium Finance Integrations
